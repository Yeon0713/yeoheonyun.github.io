These courses have provided me with a solid foundation in both theoretical and practical aspects of computer science.

Outside of academics, I have a range of hobbies that I am passionate about. I enjoy bowling, skiing, and gaming. HOBBY

I enjoy various activities in my free time. My favorite hobbies are bowling, skiing, and gaming. Here are some moments from these activities:

Bowling

Bowling is one of my favorite leisure activities. I started bowling as a fun activity with friends, but over time, it has become more than just a hobby. I often participate in local bowling tournaments and enjoy the competitive spirit. One of my most memorable moments was when I bowled a perfect game, achieving a score of 300. The precision and consistency required in bowling have taught me a lot about focus and discipline. I love the camaraderie and the excitement that comes with each game.

Skiing

Skiing is another activity that I am passionate about. The thrill of gliding down snow-covered slopes is unparalleled. I usually plan ski trips with friends and family during the winter season. One of my favorite skiing destinations is the Alpensia Resort in Pyeongchang, South Korea, which hosted the 2018 Winter Olympics. Skiing has not only been a source of excitement but also a great way to stay fit and appreciate nature. The skills required for skiing, such as balance and coordination, have positively impacted my overall physical abilities.

Gaming

Gaming has been a significant part of my life since childhood. It started as a simple pastime but has grown into a serious hobby. I enjoy a variety of games, including strategy, role-playing, and first-person shooters. Gaming has helped me develop quick decision-making skills and strategic thinking. One of my proudest achievements was reaching a high rank in a popular multiplayer game, which required teamwork and excellent communication skills. Gaming also provides a way to connect with friends from around the world, creating a global network of fellow gamers.
